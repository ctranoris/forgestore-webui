Welcome to FORGEStore: a companion for your FORGEBox!

The place to browse and share FORGEBox offerings!

 

FORGEStore is a marketplace for your FORGEBox installation.Our repository contains shared widgets, FORGEBox services, FIRE adapters and shared interactive courses. All our items are developed by the FORGE team members as well as several third party organizations that are interested to create and share learning material for FIRE experimentation. If you don't know what FORGEBox is, please visit our forgebox.eu website.
